Vocabulary Builder

Learn new words every day with their meaning and usage in real-life contexts.

  • Improve: to make something better. Example: I want to improve my English skills.
  • Journey: the act of traveling from one place to another. Example: Her journey to success was not easy.
  • Confident: feeling sure about your abilities. Example: He is confident when speaking English.

Grammar Essentials

Understand the basic rules of English grammar to improve your writing and speaking skills.

  • Simple Present: Used for habits and facts. Example: She walks to school every day.
  • Present Continuous: Actions happening now. Example: They are watching a movie.
  • Past Tense: Actions that happened in the past. Example: He visited Bali last year.

Learning Tips

  • Practice speaking with friends or in front of a mirror.
  • Watch English movies with subtitles.
  • Read English books, articles, or comics you enjoy.
  • Write a daily journal in English.
  • Listen to English songs and try to understand the lyrics.
